About TPC Pharma & Chemicals

Built on integrity and confidentiality. We operate with precision, consistency, and trust.

Company Overview

Headquartered in Mumbai, India, TPC Pharma & Chemicals operates at the intersection of quality and reliability. As a specialized pharmaceutical API and chemical trading enterprise, we provide critical supply chain solutions designed exclusively for institutional, government, and large-scale manufacturing entities.

We understand that institutional procurement is defined by absolute adherence to regulatory standards, rigorous verification, and unwavering timeline management. Through stringent vetting processes and profound market intelligence, we deliver raw materials exactly when and how they are required.
